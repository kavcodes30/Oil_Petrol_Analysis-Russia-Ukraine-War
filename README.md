
# Oil & Petrol Market Analysis During Global Conflict Periods

## The Story Behind This Project

Wars do not only affect borders and politics — they also shake economies, industries, transportation systems, and everyday life.

One of the first markets to react during geopolitical conflicts is the global oil market. Rising tensions, disrupted supply chains, sanctions, and uncertainty often lead to sharp fluctuations in crude oil and petrol prices worldwide.

This project explores how war periods influence oil and petrol markets through data analysis and visualization using Python.

Using historical datasets and exploratory data analysis techniques, the project investigates:
- Crude oil price volatility
- Petrol price comparisons across countries
- Market behavior during geopolitical instability
- Economic patterns observed during conflict timelines

---

## Project Objectives

The primary goal of this analysis is to understand how global conflict events impact fuel markets and economic stability.

Key objectives include:
- Analyzing crude oil price trends over time
- Comparing petrol prices across different countries
- Studying market reactions during war periods
- Visualizing fuel market fluctuations
- Identifying patterns and correlations in historical data
